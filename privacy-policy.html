Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Devrim ONCU ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile applications, specifically DeepMotion AI, SpeakEasy AI, and Football Analyzer.

1. Biometric & Face Data Processing (DeepMotion AI)

ZERO RETENTION POLICY: DeepMotion AI uses advanced Artificial Intelligence to map facial geometry solely for the purpose of generating a "face-swap" video.

• We DO NOT store, share, or sell your facial data.
• Your original photo and the generated biometric map are processed securely via encrypted cloud functions and are permanently deleted immediately after the video is generated.
• We do not use your face data to train our AI models.

2. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous device identifiers (such as Android ID) to manage your in-app credits and prevent fraud.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our apps (e.g., buttons clicked, app crashes) to improve performance.
  • Payment Information: All in-app purchases are securely processed by Stripe. We do not collect or store your credit card numbers on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our apps. Their privacy policies govern their use of your data:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure cloud processing and crash analytics.
  • Stripe: Used for payment processing.
  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying personalized or non-personalized ads (depending on your consent).

4. Advertising and Analytics (AdMob & GDPR)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Depending on your region (e.g., EEA/UK), you will be presented with a User Messaging Platform (UMP) consent dialog to choose whether you accept personalized advertisements.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since we do not store personal accounts or retain biometric data, there is no personal profile to delete. If you wish to reset your anonymous device ID, you can clear the app data from your Android settings or uninstall the app.
